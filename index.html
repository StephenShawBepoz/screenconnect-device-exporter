ScreenConnect Device Exporter

A Chrome extension that exports device details from ScreenConnect to CSV — including hardware specs, RAM, OS, and session info.

Download Extension (.zip)

Installation

1

Download and unzip

Click the download button above and extract the zip file to a folder on your computer.

2

Open Chrome Extensions

Navigate to chrome://extensions in your browser and enable Developer mode (toggle in the top right).

3

Load the extension

Click Load unpacked and select the folder you extracted in step 1.

4

Pin it (optional)

Click the puzzle piece icon in Chrome's toolbar, then pin ScreenConnect Device Exporter for easy access.

How to Use

1

Navigate to your device group

Open ScreenConnect and go to the Access page. Select the session group containing the devices you want to export.

2

Scroll to load all devices

ScreenConnect lazy-loads rows. Scroll through the full device list to make sure all devices are rendered before exporting.

3

Click Export to CSV

Click the extension icon in the toolbar, then press Export to CSV. The extension will click through each device to collect full hardware details.

4

Wait for the scan to complete

The progress bar shows how many devices have been scanned. A CSV file will download automatically when finished. You can click Stop at any time to export what's been collected so far.
